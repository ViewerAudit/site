About Viewer Audit

Viewer Audit is a powerful tool designed to help identify potential viewbotting on streaming platforms like Twitch and Kick. Our advanced algorithms analyze multiple engagement metrics to provide accurate botting likelihood scores.

How It Works

We analyze various metrics including viewer-to-chatter ratios, engagement patterns, follower counts, and chat activity to determine the likelihood of viewbotting. Our system compares these metrics against normal patterns to identify suspicious activity.

Metrics Analyzed

  • Live viewer count vs active chatters
  • Chat message rate and quality
  • Follower-to-viewer ratios
  • Stream duration patterns
  • Engagement consistency

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the botting detection?

Our algorithm analyzes multiple factors and provides a likelihood score. While highly accurate, it should be used as a tool for investigation rather than definitive proof.

What data do you collect?

We only collect publicly available streaming data. No personal information is stored, and favorites are saved locally on your device.

Can I compare channels across platforms?

Yes! You can compare Twitch and Kick channels side by side to analyze differences in engagement patterns.

How often is the data updated?

Data is updated in real-time as you search. We pull live data from the platforms' APIs to ensure accuracy.
